Getting here

From Seoul · KTX

KTX from Seoul Station to Gangneung Station takes approximately 2 hours. From Gangneung Station, take a taxi (~15 minutes) to KIST Gangneung Institute, or transfer to local bus 202 / 300 toward Saimdang-ro.

By car

Take Yeongdong Expressway and exit at Gangneung IC, then follow signs toward Saimdang-ro and the KIST campus. Visitor parking is available at the Research Building (L1) entrance — please notify ahead by email.
